Joshua Powell

Meet Josh:

Josh is a driven and accomplished individual with a strong foundation in education, leadership, and diverse professional experiences. Currently pursuing a Business degree at Brigham Young University, Josh has consistently demonstrated his commitment to academic excellence, maintaining an impressive GPA of 3.86. He is an active member of the Finance Society and the Private Equity/Venture Capital Association, reflecting his keen interest in business, start-ups and Finance.

Josh's journey in leadership and entrepreneurship began long before his tenure at Southern Virginia University, where he attained a perfect GPA of 4.0 and earned a place on the President's List, showcasing his dedication to academic success. His entrepreneurial spirit shines through as he has been the owner of one business and the manager of two other family-owned businesses, honing his skills in management and strategic decision-making.

Josh's versatility extends to technical expertise as a Technician and Appraiser at Doctor Clean Carpet Cleaning, and his experience as an Organic Horticultural Specialist at Risen Labs & Gold Naturals demonstrates his dedication to sustainability and innovation.

In his role as Student Body President Student Body Government, Josh displayed exceptional leadership, communication, event planning, budget management, and conflict resolution skills. He left an indelible mark as the first sophomore ever in Aptos High's Student Body Leadership. Josh's commitment to service was further demonstrated during his 2 year voluntary representative service with The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints.

Beyond his impressive professional and academic accomplishments, Josh is a multifaceted individual with a passion for athletics, including his competing in NCAA-DIII football and being selected to California's All-State Football Team. He has received the National Football Foundation and College Hall of Fame Scholar Athlete Award, showcasing his dedication both on and off the field.

In summary, Josh is a dynamic individual who combines a strong educational foundation, diverse professional experiences, leadership abilities, and a commitment to service and excellence. His entrepreneurial spirit, dedication to teamwork, and passion for personal growth make him a valuable asset to any organization or endeavor he undertakes.
